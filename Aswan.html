Philae Temple

A stunning ancient temple dedicated to the goddess Isis, located on Agilkia Island. It is known for its beautiful carvings and was relocated to protect it from the rising Nile waters.

Abu Simbel Temples

Two massive rock-cut temples built by Pharaoh Ramses II, famous for their colossal statues and the unique solar alignment phenomenon twice a year.

A modern engineering marvel that controls the Nile’s flow, providing hydroelectric power and preventing floods. It also created Lake Nasser, one of the world's largest artificial lakes.
